Installing Addons

Video​

The second half of this video shows exaclty how to install addons.

Step-by-Step​

  1. Find a plugin or theme that interests you on the BetterDiscord website.
  2. Download that plugin or theme to your computer.
  3. In Discord, go to your BetterDiscord settings and select either the Plugins or Themes tab depending on what you chose in the first step.
  4. At the top click the Open XXXXXX Folder button.
  5. Drag and drop, or move, the plugin or theme you downloaded into this folder.
  6. Go back to your Plugins or Themes pages and enable your plugin or theme.
